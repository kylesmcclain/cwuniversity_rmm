Overview

The site-level Passwords tab area enables the creation and storage of passwords that allow the NOC and Help Desk Services to access client machines for troubleshooting and other support.

Note: You can use either the Passwords tab or the Vault to store passwords, but both must be managed and kept up-to-date.

Types of passwords include:

Servers
Desktops
Lights Out Management
Pre-Defined and Custom Applications
Custom Passwords & Properties (i.e. email, QuickBooks, etc.)
vPro

The Passwords tab also lets you configure Access Control groups and store vault encryption passphrases for retrieval.

The Server Passwords section can only be completed for sites with installed server agents. For workgroup sites, a common local administrator account must be documented with Desktop Passwords. For all third-party application credentials and any credentials not covered by the Passwords tab or Network Info site-level tab, use Custom Passwords & Properties.
