Follow the steps documented below to launch ScreenConnect. 

How to Launch ScreenConnect
Navigate to the Endpoint > Devices.
Locate the machine that you want to launch ScreenConnect on.
Under Log In, choose either ScreenConnect Remote Access or ScreenConnect Backstage.



Note: The same launch options are available in the device details page.
