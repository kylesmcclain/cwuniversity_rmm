Overview

Notification Rules (in Settings > Setup > RMM > Notification Rules) specify how you would like the NOC to handle alerts received from your RMM agents based on criticality, service level, and date/time. Rules must be set up for an entire week. The Notifications window displays the agent’s service level, site count, created by date/time/user, and modified by date/time/user details.
