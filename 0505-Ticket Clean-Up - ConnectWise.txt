Overview

The Ticket Clean-Up tool helps you close tickets in bulk. Filters can be applied to close tickets for a particular Site or Device.

Create a Ticket Clean-Up Job

To create a ticket clean-up job:

Navigate to Service Delivery > Tickets (Legacy) > Ticket Clean-Up.
Select your Site.
Select the Resource for which the tickets exist.
You can assign this job to the NOC, the Help Desk Service or your partner.
Specify a particular date (mandatory) before which the ticket clean-up can be performed.
Enter a reason (mandatory) as to why you want the tickets to be deleted.
To create the job, click Generate Ticket Clean-Up job.

Note: The tickets will be deleted (job will be executed) during non-business hours. It will take a minimum 12 hours to execute the job.
