Overview

We have elevated key device and compliance information for MS Intune managed devices on the Integration page on the Devices Summary page. This page will provide actionable information (identify out of compliance devices in Intune) for partner technicians to log in MS Intune and take necessary corrective measures for devices out of compliance.

The devices that will be mapped from MS Intune will belong to one of the following Operating Systems:

Windows 10 version 1709 (RS3) and later, Windows 8.1 RT, PCs running Windows 8.1 (Sustaining mode)

macOS 10.15 and later

The Intune data is fetched on an interval of one hour once a connection with a tenant is established successfully.

The Intune information can be viewed by navigating to Endpoints > Devices > Integrations > Microsoft Intune.

Intune devices can be currently filtered by using the filter option on the right hand side panel.
