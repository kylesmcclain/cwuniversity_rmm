Overview

ConnectWise RMM has a Network Monitoring and Management solution that provides a single pane of glass experience for managing your Network Devices through the Asio Platform. The Network Monitoring Solution is fully integrated with the RMM and Asio Platform. Currently, this solution is only available to RMM Premium and Pro partners who have an SSO instance of the Asio Platform and have access to Companies and Sites.

We offer three levels for this solution:

Basic Network Monitoring
Advanced Network Monitoring
Premier Network Monitoring

Premier and Advanced Network Monitoring are add-on products, enabled at the site level. For an additional cost, you can upgrade your embedded Network Monitoring capabilities. For more information on the products, review the following documentation.
