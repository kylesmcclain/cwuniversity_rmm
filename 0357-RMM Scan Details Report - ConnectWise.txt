RMM Scan Details Report
Overview

The RMM Scan Details Report lists the IP range and deployment schedules to scan the machines in the network for the agent.

 

To locate this screen, navigate to 

Reports > Asio > Detailed Reports > Agents > RMM Scan Details Report.

 

Note: Only agent schedules configured in the RMM Scanning Tool will be reported here.

RMM Scan Details Report

Click RMM Scan History to get a detailed list of agent deployment history.
