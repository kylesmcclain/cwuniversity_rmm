Overview

This article covers the recommended settings to employ for your Communicator Policy. These best practice settings for different RMM configurations are based on the feedback and analysis of policies used by partners.

Policy Details
Policy Usage	Communicator policies allow the partner to define the list of options available on the system tray menu for endpoints.
Policy Type	Communicator
Policy Name	Evolve Recommended - Communicator Policy
Policy Applies To	

These settings will be applied to the following types of agents:

RMM Enabled Windows Desktop

RMM Enabled MAC Desktop

RMM Enabled Windows Server
