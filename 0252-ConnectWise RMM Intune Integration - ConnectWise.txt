Overview

Integration with Microsoft® Intune allows multi-tenant management of customer end-points in ConnectWise RMM™. This solution has simplified the complex and powerful MS Intune solution within RMM, making it intuitive and easy to use.

Identify Intune managed devices in ConnectWise RMM
Device Compliance at a Tenant and Device level
Intune device compliance and configuration information on the Devices page and device level details
Intune managed applications information on the Devices page
Ability to sync Intune devices with MS Intune
Multi-Tenant Device Compliance Policy Management for MS Intune Devices
Multi-Tenant Device Configuration Profile Management for MS Intune Devices
Alerts for critical events for MS Intune managed devices
Requirements
ConnectWise RMM Pro or Premium.
Primary Admins and Primary Super Admins have access to initiate and configure integrations by default.
Admins must assign Integrations permissions via an Application Role to enable access to configure the integration and view Intune on the Devices page. Refer to Application Roles to learn how to customize permissions and application roles and assign them to users in ConnectWise Home.
To set up this integration in the Asio platform, navigate to Integrations > Asio Integrations > Network Management and select Microsoft Intune.
