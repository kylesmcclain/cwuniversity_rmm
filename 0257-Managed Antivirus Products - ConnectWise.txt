Overview

Antivirus (AV) vendors often release software updates. Managed AV products are hard-coded into the ConnectWise RMM agent so we can identify the AV product when updates are received and check the definition file to make any needed changes. Identified AV products are those not yet hard-coded in our agent, so we identify the AV product with our data center only and do not provide a definition update (meaning we do not yet manage these products).

Note: Since our agent is updated quarterly, it cannot include all of the latest AV product updates.
