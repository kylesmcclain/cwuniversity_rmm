Access Levels at a Glance
Overview

NOC access levels (0-3) designate how you would like proactive troubleshooting for your servers, depending on RMM Servers level. For example, you might give the NOC permission to log in to the server and diagnose the problem but not fix anything until they contact you (Access Level 1). Or you might give the NOC full permission to diagnose and fix issues as needed without contacting you (Access Level 3).
