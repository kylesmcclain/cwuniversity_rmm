Using the Chat Support Feature
Last updated
Jan 15, 2025

No headers
Log in to the Connectwise Security Support Portal.
Once logged in, you are routed to the Home page. Click the Chat Feature in the bottom right-hand corner to initiate your chat session.


You are asked if you are experiencing an emergency security incident, select Yes or No.


You are then connected with a member of the ConnectWise security team who will assist you with your issue.
