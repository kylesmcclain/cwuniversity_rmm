Overview 

This page provides information on the latest enhancements and features for ConnectWise RMM.

Announcements
Legacy Pages and reports will be deprecated in early January.

The following legacy reports, pages, and options will not be visible to any users:

Reports > Asio > Custom Reports
Reports > Asio > Scripting
Reports > Asio > Audits > Intellimon Alert Suspension report
Reports > Asio > Backup >Mac Time Machine Backup Report
Chat button on Top Navigation pane
Help Desk Services will be offline on December 12th.

Our Help Desk Services Partner Line Chat, NOC Service Partner Chat and our BCDR Service Partner Chat will be offline for a few days as we transition to a new platform.

During this period, if you require assistance with the Help Desk Service, please reach out to our Partner Care Team via email at ExpertServicesHelpDesk@connectwise.com or by phone at +1 (855) 342-8432 (Toll-Free) or +1 (628) 210-7510 (Local).

NOC and BCDR by opening a ticket through the portal or by calling (+1) 914-219-1881 and selecting the appropriate team

We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and appreciate your understanding as we work to enhance our services for you.
