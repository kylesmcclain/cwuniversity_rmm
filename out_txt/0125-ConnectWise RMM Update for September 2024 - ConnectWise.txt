Announcements  
Left Navigation Menu Realignment

On October 1, 2024, the left-hand navigation within the ConnectWise Asio™ platform will be changing, offering an enhanced navigation experience, below are some of the changes you can expect to see in your menu: 

Consolidation of the current Devices, Quick Access, Alerting, Patching, and Communicator screens under a new Endpoints menu 

Creation of a new Integrations menu 

Moving the Policies and Packages screens from Settings into Endpoints 

Moving the BrightGauge menu into the Reports menu 

Moving the Tickets menu into a new Service Delivery menu 

Additional menu and screen names will be updated for clarity 

No action is required on your part; however, we do suggest familiarizing yourself with these changes and preparing your teams to limit navigation challenges or confusion in the days following the release.

For more information regarding these changes, please review the Left Navigation Menu changes FAQ.
