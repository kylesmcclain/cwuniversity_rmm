Overview

Complete ITSupport Portal setup is an essential element that contributes to your success with the Help Desk. The information included in the portal allows the Help Desk to easily find specific details regarding each site and act immediately to handle a request.

Use the sections below to understand and complete the steps required to successfully activate Help Desk for your client sites in the ITSupport Portal:

Select Help Desk Product Options.
Create a Help Desk Notification Rule.
Provide Help Desk Information.
Add Required Documentation and Credentials.
Confirm Completion and Set Client Expectations.
