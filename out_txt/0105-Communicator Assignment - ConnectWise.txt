Communicator Assignment
Last updated
Jun 27, 2025
Table of contents
No headers

The communicator tray could be assigned to endpoints in two ways:

Packages:

Create a Communicator Policy: Endpoints > Policies > Add
Create a new package or add the Corresponding Communicator Policy to an existing package.
Assign the package with a Communicator Policy to the required site.
Communicators will be assigned to all endpoints within the site.

For more information refer to the Policies and Packages article.

Device Details:

Go to Endpoints > Devices > Select the endpoint you want to assign the communicator to > Settings > Devices.
Select the default Communicator Policy.
Enable the Communicator.
Communicators will be assigned only to the exact endpoint.
