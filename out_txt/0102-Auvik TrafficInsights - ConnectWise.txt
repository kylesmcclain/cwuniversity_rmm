Overview

Auvik TrafficInsights™, a new network bandwidth monitoring feature, is now available. TrafficInsights provides visibility into applications used on the network, the users of those applications, and where the data is going. The feature is planned to be available for an additional cost published separately, but for now, is offered free of charge and can be enabled from the Auvik portal.

For more information, see Auvik TrafficInsights.
