Overview

Your continued communication with ConnectWise Help Desk Services will make your client's experience a success. Knowing how to properly escalate Help Desk tickets and manage ticket queues reduces the time it takes to troubleshoot and resolve issues.

To report issues with the Help Desk, use the ConnectWise Asio® Support page. This page allows you to contact the Help Desk team leads via chat.

How to Contact Help Desk Services
Select Support (top-right) in the Asio platform.


Click Chat Now in the bottom-right corner on the next screen.

Note: The chat is available Monday - Friday from 8 AM to 8 PM EST. 

Fill out the required information with your contact details and then select the following from the dropdowns to get routed to the Help Desk Service Partner Line Team:


PSA Tickets

For issues with any Professional Services Automation (PSA) Integration, transmit tickets via select vendors, such as ConnectWise and Autotask. For details, see PSA Integration in the ConnectWise University.
