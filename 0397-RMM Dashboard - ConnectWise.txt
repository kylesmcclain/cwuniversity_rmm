Overview

The ConnectWise RMM dashboard provides a centralized view of all of your clients' networks and devices, giving you real-time visibility into the performance and status of these systems. This allows you to quickly identify and resolve issues before they become major problems.

The RMM dashboard is located under  Dashboards > RMM.

Using the RMM Dashboard
Filters

The RMM Dashboard consists of two global filters, Companies and Sites. You can apply these filters to update and refresh the entire dashboard:

Navigate to Dashboards > RMM.
Click Filters.

 
Select a Company. Optionally, select a Site to narrow down your results. You can select multiple companies and sites.

Click Show Results to populate the dashboard with your results.
Refresh

You can click on the refresh button, and the information in the dashboard will be refreshed without losing the applied filters. The page also displays the last refresh time.


Dashboard Pods

RMM Dashboard has the following pods available in the system to display a holistic view of the data and information on a single page.

Workstations and Servers

The Workstations and Servers pod displays the number the managed endpoints by your RMM. It consists of the Windows PC, Windows Server, Mac, Linux Server, and VMWare Devices. Data for the managed devices is fetched in real time to display on the pod. The total devices shown depends on the user's permission and resource assignment.

Drilldown

Click on the counts for the different devices to be redirected to the Devices page with the appropriate filters applied. This will display all of the devices that are found in those counts.

Network Devices

The Network Devices pod lists all of the managed network endpoints by your RMM using the Network Monitoring feature. It consists of the routers, switches, firewalls, generic network devices, and other network monitors. Data for the managed devices is fetched in real time to display on the pod. The total devices shown depends on the user's permission and resource assignment.

Drilldown

Click on the counts for the different network devices to be redirected to the Network devices page with the appropriate filters applied. This will display all of the devices that are found in those counts.

Cloud Devices

The Cloud Devices pod lists all of the managed cloud infrastructure endpoints in your RMM using Cloud Monitoring and displays their status. Data for the managed devices is fetched in real time and displayed on the pod. The total number of devices shown depends on the user's permission and resource assignment.

Drilldown

By clicking on the counts for the different cloud devices, you will be redirected to the Cloud devices page with the appropriate filters applied. This will display all of the devices that are found in those counts.

Workstation Status

The Workstation Status pod lists all of the workstation endpoints managed by your RMM and their online or offline status. The data is broken into graphs based on the workstation OS type (Windows or macOS). Data for the managed devices' availability is fetched in real time and is displayed in the pod. The total devices shown depends on the user's permission and resource assignment.


Drilldown
Click on the counts on the percentage chart.
You are redirected to the Devices page with appropriate filters applied. This will display the devices that make up the Workstation Status percentage.

Workstation Patching Compliance

The Workstation Patching Compliance pod lists displays the patch compliance status of the workstation endpoints managed by your RMM. It displays the status of your Windows Desktop and macOs endpoints. Data for this pod is fetched in real time. The chart is broken into different sections based on how compliant your endpoints are. You can select the different percentages to filter them in your chart.
