Overview

We have added the ability to set up a secure working connection between ConnectWise RMM and tenants managed in MS Intune, by integrating with your Cloud Solutions Provider (CSP) account. A one-time secure setup with the CSP account enables the integration of MS Intune tenants with CW RMM. 

Note:

The Microsoft Intune integration only works with a CSP account.
Our Integration only provides support for customers with a reseller-type relationship. This means that in the Microsoft Intune sites dropdown for mapping, you will only see customers who have a reseller-type relationship.

The following process illustrates the steps required to set up a new integration with a CSP account: 

Setup
Navigate to Integrations > Asio Integrations.
Enable the Microsoft Intune integration.

Enter a Connection Name and click Generate Token.

You will be redirected to a Microsoft Sign-in page. Enter your credentials for Microsoft CSP Account.
A token will be automatically generated for you and will redirect you to the Marketplace Integration page.
Click Test Connection.
Once the connection is successful, click Save and Proceed.
Map your ConnectWise Sites to Microsoft Tenants. Only choose sites with Microsoft Intune tenants.
You can also leverage the Find Matches feature that will automatically try and match the Company/Site record with the corresponding site names in the Microsoft Intune sites.
Click Save and Proceed.
Click Continue to confirm the number of mapped sites.
Click Complete Setup.
Disable Intune Integration

You can disable (delete) the Intune integration by navigating to Integrations > Asio Integrations.

Next to the Intune Integration, click Disable. Click Submit.

Alerting for Not-Compliant Devices

An alert will be generated for the partner when a device goes to a Not-Compliant state in MS Intune. This alert will be auto-closed when the device moves out of the Not-Compliant state.
