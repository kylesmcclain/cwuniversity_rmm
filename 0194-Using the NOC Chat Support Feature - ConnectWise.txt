Overview

This document describes the process for starting a chat with ConnectWise NOC Services™ through the ConnectWise Asio® portal.

Getting Started

Before attempting to create a chat session, ensure that you are using ConnectWise Single Sign-On (SSO) and that you have enabled the new Asio ticket experience. This enables ticket creation through chat sessions.

Available Support Teams

NOC Services chat allows for real-time assistance related to data protection, network monitoring and management, and server and desktop incident escalation. Experts are available to answer your questions 24/7.

Partners that are already familiar with the process can use the links below to jump directly to the NOC Chat support page for their region:

NA partners: https://control.itsupport247.net/QADashB/ContactUs
EU partners: https://euplatform.connectwise.com/QADashB/ContactUs
AU partners: https://auplatform.connectwise.com/QADashB/ContactUs 
Contact NOC Services via Chat

Follow the steps below to contact NOC from within Asio:

In the top-right corner of the Asio portal, click Support.

Click the CHAT NOW button in the bottom-right corner of the ConnectWise Service & Support page. 
In the chat flyout, complete the following fields:

Note: There is a brief wait before the chat window loads.

First Name
Last Name
Primary Email
Support Area: Select Using ConnectWise Products & Experiencing Technical Issues.
Type: Select Technical Issues.
Product Line: Select BCDR or NOC Services.
Subject: Enter a brief description of your issue.

Click Next.
On the next screen, click the appropriate Support link for your region:
NA: https://control.itsupport247.net/QADashB/ContactUs
EU: https://euplatform.connectwise.com/QADashB/ContactUs
AU: https://auplatform.connectwise.com/QADashB/ContactUs

On the Support page, under What can we help you with?, select the appropriate category for support.
Click the second drop-down to select the subcategory for support

Click Start a chat.

On the Expert Services Chat screen, complete the following fields:
Subject: Provide a short, high-level description of your issue.
Description: Provide additional details about your issue.
Company Name: This must match your Asio Company name.
Site Name: This must match the site's Asio Site name.
Name: Enter your first and last name.
Email: Enter the email address associated with ConnectWise Home and SSO.
Phone: Enter the phone number associated with ConnectWise Home.

Click Start Chat Session.
Click the minimized chat window to open it.


Important: Do not click the chat pop-out icon. This abandons your current chat and creates a new chat.


The next available engineer will address your issue.
After the issue has been addressed, click End in the top-right corner of the chat window.

If you want to leave feedback about your chat experience, click Yes! Otherwise, click Not today.
