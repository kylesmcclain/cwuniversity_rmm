Autotask Integration Reporting on Synchronization Status
Last updated
Aug 16, 2024
Table of contents
Overview
Overview

Use this feature to identify and fix sync issues if the initial configuration fails testing or if you experience sync issues at any point after completing the integration. Click on The View Sync Status Reports button to view the summary sync status of each category. A Details option allows you to drill down for additional information on individual sites, tickets, and assets.
