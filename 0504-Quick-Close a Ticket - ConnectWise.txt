How to Quick-Close a Ticket
Overview

Quick-closing a ticket allows you to close out a ticket immediately without making updates or filling out the information in the Work Status Update ticket. When quick-closing a ticket, a blank Work Status Update ticket is created, which you can update later.

How to Quick-Close a Ticket
Browse to the queue and to the ticket container.
Select Tickets by ticking the checkbox next to the ticket ID.
Right-click to open the ticket menu. Select Quick Close.
Verify that these are the tickets you want to close. A warning message box is displayed. Click OK to close the selected ticket.
