Overview

Overall summary of the percentage of BCDR Continuity backups that have succeeded or failed.

Endpoint and URI

https://itsapi.itsupport247.net/repo.../agent/details

Method

POST

Request Body

{

"Month":4,

"SiteCode":"AGTSolutions",

"Year":2015,

}

Response Body

{

"error":null,

"percentFailed":16.67,

"percentOther":0.00,

"percentSucceeded":83.33

}

Parameter Descriptions for BCDR Continuity Backup Summary
Parameter	Description
"Month"	Requested month (numerical)
"SiteCode"	Unique site code listed in Asio™ Platform (ITSupport Portal)
"Year"	Requested year
"percentFailed"	Percent of failed backups in the month and year requested
"percentOther"	Percent of backups neither failed nor succeeded in the month and year requested
"percentSucceeded"	Percent of successful backups in the month and year requested
Source of Data in the Asio Platform 

Reports > Asio > Executive Report > Recover Continuity Backup Data > Backup Status > Overview
