Overview

This article covers the recommended settings to employ for your OS Patching Desktop Policy. These best practice settings for different RMM configurations are based on the feedback and analysis of policies used by partners.

Policy Details
Policy Usage	OS Patching Desktop policies allow the partner to define how the Desktop endpoints are patched by the RMM Agent.
Policy Type	OS Patching Desktop
Policy Name	Evolve Recommended - OS Patching Desktop Policy
Policy Applies To	

These settings will be applied to the following types of agents:

RMM Enabled Windows Desktop

RMM Enabled MAC Desktop
