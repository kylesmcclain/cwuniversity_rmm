Overview

Antivirus (AV) and endpoint protection vendors often release software updates. Managed AV products are hard-coded into the RMM agent, allowing us to identify the AV product when updates are received and verify the definition file to make any necessary changes. Conversely, identified AV products (below) are not yet hard-coded in our agent, so we identify the AV product with our data center only and do not provide a definition update (meaning we do not yet manage these products).
